    Our Story

    Quixote Home + Farm + Ranch, took its inspiration from Cervantes’ novel.

    The primary character, Don Quixote, had a noble but unusual perspective of the world. Most people thought he was a “poquito” crazy, but he saw the best in people. He saw beyond their external selves to who they were in the eternal. We think this is a trait to aspire to.

    We purchased this farm as a way to care for our parents, children, and hopefully, our future grandchildren. Some people think we are a bit crazy, and perhaps we are.

    Additionally, we believe our great privilege means it is our duty to come alongside the voiceless and dis-empowered-by using our gifting and talents. We are a welcoming ranch.


    Lara's childhood in Northern Spain gave her a love for food, all things Spanish, Southern French, rustic and well-traveled.
    Husband Skip retired from a lifetime in service to others, making his side gig in construction and landscape design his main gig.

    Quixote Farm brings together their many loves. It is a humbling, challenging project, but the rewards are great!

    Giving Back

    For the Children is building a world free of family-induced childhood trauma.

    For The Children promotes physical, emotional and spiritual healing for child victims of neglect, abuse, and abandonment through intervention programs, education and advocacy
